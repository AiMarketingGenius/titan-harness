The Alex widget in action

This demo page is a staging surface for the AMG chatbot widget that will ship on aimarketinggenius.io. The floating button lower-right opens a conversation with Alex — your AMG Business Coach agent, project-backed with the full Encyclopedia v1.3 context + live client_facts lookup.

Try these prompts

The widget connects to Atlas via the agent_context_loader tool. Alex's full KB is loaded + cached (5-min prompt caching) so responses stay crisp and on-brand.

"What does the Chamber AI Advantage program include?"

"How does Revere's 18% rev-share compare to the standard rate?"

"I'm a local business owner. Where do I start?"

Integration

Single-script embed on aimarketinggenius.io:

<script src="https://amg-cdn.example.com/widget.js"></script>

Backend: /api/alex/message on atlas-api (project-backed via agent_context_loader).
