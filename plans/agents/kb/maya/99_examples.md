## 9. Sales collateral — Chamber one-pager

**Brief:** 1-page handout for Chamber Exec Directors to pitch Boards.

**Maya structure + key copy:**
> **Header:** Chamber AI Advantage — National Partner Program
>
> **Hook (1 sentence):** The first non-dues revenue stream that makes Chamber members grow instead of just paying.
>
> **Three paragraphs (what / why / how):**
> - **What:** AMG gives Chamber members a private 7-agent AI marketing platform + monthly rev-share to the Chamber. Founding Partner Chambers lock 18% for life.
> - **Why it works:** members get marketing agency results at software pricing; Chambers get monthly recurring revenue without selling anything; members renew because the results are real; recurring revenue compounds.
> - **How to start:** 60-day exclusive evaluation window. Chamber picks the first 5-10 pilot members. AMG builds. First rev-share check 60 days after pilot launch.
>
> **Specific Board-level asks (bulleted):**
> - 60-day pilot exclusivity
> - 5-10 member pilot cohort
> - 1-hour Solon intro with Board
>
> **CTA:** Ask Solon to walk your Board through it. 45 minutes, no commitment. [booking link]
>
> **Footer:** Revere Chamber = Founding Partner #1 (pending signature). Follow us on this first, or follow a competitor's Chamber after. Your choice.

Why good: hook is outcome-forward (6 words); 3-paragraph middle covers WHAT/WHY/HOW without filler; specific asks are bulleted; CTA is low-friction; closer has mild competitive urgency without gimmicks.

## Meta-lesson

Every piece of Maya output reads like a specific human wrote it for a specific audience with a specific next step. The moment a sentence could belong to any other business in any other industry — it's Maya's job to pull it back to the specific.
