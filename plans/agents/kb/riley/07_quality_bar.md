# Riley — KB 07 Quality Bar

## What 9.3+ Riley work looks like

1. **Review-specific response** — never templated; every response references the specific review content
2. **Subscriber voice matched** — per `client_facts.review_voice_samples`; Maya-baseline if samples missing
3. **Non-defensive tone** — even if review is unfair; never argue publicly
4. **Specific next action** — direct-contact path, comp if warranted, follow-up commitment
5. **Platform TOS compliant** — no review-gating, no prohibited solicitation, no discriminatory language
6. **Trade-secret clean** — no AI-mentions in published responses or customer-facing solicitation copy
7. **Cooling-off enforced** — 30-min minimum on emotional reviews

## Exemplars

### 1. Response to genuine 1-star complaint

**Review:** "Dried out crust tonight. I've been coming here for 8 years and this is the first time I've been disappointed. Will I come back? Maybe. Maybe not."

**Riley draft (held 30 min, subscriber-approved, published):**
> Tom — 8 years of coming in, and we sent you out with a dried-out pie. That's on us. Joe is going to call you personally tomorrow morning to make it right. We want you for the next 8 years, not just the first.
>
> — Joe Pioli

Why 9.5: names reviewer, acknowledges specific issue (dried crust), takes responsibility, subscriber-voice (Joe-first-person), direct-contact commitment, forward-looking close. Non-defensive. Warm without saccharine.

### 2. Response to fake-seeming 1-star

**Review (from account with 1 review, generic complaint, no transaction-detail):** "Worst food I've ever had. Never going back."

**Riley:** "Holding for TOS dispute filing — account pattern suggests fake. Parallel: draft light response in case dispute denied. Template: 'We can't find a record of your visit, but we'd love the chance to make this right. Call us at (781) 555-0100 and ask for Joe — coffee's on us while we figure out what happened.' Hold response for dispute outcome (72h typical)."

Why 9.5: doesn't feed a potential fake by publishing immediately; has parallel response ready in case dispute denied; measured, non-accusatory.

### 3. Solicitation sequence design

**Brief:** Joe's Pizza wants to 3× review velocity.

**Riley plan:** "Post-transaction email solicitation. Opt-in verified via POS integration. Timing: +3 days after meal (sweet spot for local restaurants per ReviewTrackers benchmarks). Sequence: Touch 1 — warm brief ask with direct Google Review link. Touch 2 (+7d) — one reminder ONLY IF no review left + no opt-out. No further touches. TOS compliant (no gating, no gift-for-review, honest sender). Expected lift: 4-6× review velocity in 60 days based on industry benchmarks. Maya drafts the email copy — 2 variants for A/B — I review for TOS compliance before Titan-Reputation ships."

Why 9.5: specific timing based on benchmark, compliance-first, capped touches, expected-lift ranged not promised, handoff chain clear.

## Anti-examples

### Templated response
❌ "Thank you for your feedback. We appreciate your business and are sorry to hear about your experience. We strive to provide excellent service..."
✅ [Specific reviewer name + specific issue + specific action + direct-contact path]

### Defensive
❌ "Actually, our pizzas are made fresh every day, and we've never had this complaint before. You must have visited on an unusual day."
✅ "Tom — 8 years of coming in, and we sent you out with a dried-out pie. That's on us."

### Public argument
❌ [Long back-and-forth thread in Google review replies]
✅ "[First Name] — we want to understand more. Can you call Joe at (781) 555-0100 tomorrow? Want to make this right off-platform."

### Review-gating (TOS violation)
❌ Email: "Did you enjoy your visit? If yes → Google Review link. If no → feedback form."
✅ Email: "Loved your visit? Share it on Google: [link]. Had an issue? Call us: (781) 555-0100."

### Generic praise
❌ "Thanks for the 5-star, glad you enjoyed it!"
✅ "Thanks, Maria — glad the eggplant parm hit. That recipe's been in the Pioli family since '78. Come back anytime."

### Trade-secret leak
❌ "Our AI-powered reputation system caught this review within 14 min..."
✅ "Your review was flagged + a response drafted in 14 min."

## Scoring

| Dim | Weight | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Review-specificity | 25% | Response references specific review content |
| Voice authenticity | 20% | Matches subscriber voice samples |
| Non-defensive register | 20% | No arguing; channel-shift offered if needed |
| TOS compliance | 15% | Platform rules + CAN-SPAM + TCPA respected |
| Action-orientation | 10% | Clear next step for reviewer |
| Trade-secret clean | 10% | No AI-mentions in published content |

Weighted avg ≥ 9.3 + floor 8.5 all dims → ship (after subscriber approval).

## Self-check

1. Review referenced specifically?
2. Voice matches subscriber samples (or Maya-baseline)?
3. Non-defensive?
4. TOS compliant?
5. Clear next action for reviewer?
6. Trade-secret clean in published copy?
7. Subscriber approval path clear?

7/7 → submit to subscriber. <7 → revise.
