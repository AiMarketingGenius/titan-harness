## Proposals — EXTRA vigilance

Contracts + proposals are signed + archived + referenced in disputes + sometimes attached in subscriber's own internal docs shared with their Board/stakeholders. Trade-secret discipline is permanent:

- ❌ "AMG's proprietary Claude-powered Atlas platform..."
- ✅ "AMG's proprietary Atlas platform..."
- ❌ "Services delivered via beast + HostHatch infrastructure"
- ✅ "Services delivered via AMG's production infrastructure"
- ❌ Technical specs enumerate: "LiteLLM gateway, Supabase pgvector, n8n queue"
- ✅ Technical specs generalize: "unified AI gateway, managed data layer, workflow automation"

## Technical-scope language in SOWs

When SOWs describe deliverables, use capability-language not vendor-language:

- ❌ "SEO analysis powered by Claude Sonnet"
- ✅ "SEO analysis powered by Atlas"
- ❌ "Voice AI via ElevenLabs"
- ✅ "Voice AI on Atlas"
- ❌ "Memory persistence via Supabase with nomic-embed-text"
- ✅ "Persistent memory layer on Atlas"

## Chamber Partnership Contract — specific considerations

Chambers will have attorneys review these. Attorneys may ask about underlying AI stack for their compliance-due-diligence. Handling:

- **In the contract itself:** "AI Marketing Genius delivers services via Atlas, its proprietary AI platform. Atlas incorporates industry-leading foundation models under commercial licenses. Specific model selection remains at AMG's discretion and may evolve as the industry advances." — truthful (we do use commercial foundation models); doesn't name them.
- **In attorney Q&A sessions:** escalate vendor-name questions to Solon + AMG counsel. Don't volunteer names; if attorney insists for compliance specificity, Solon decides.

## Subscriber-confidentiality wall

Proposals draft FOR one subscriber never reference ANOTHER subscriber's:
- Specific pricing (except per-v1.3 published rates)
- Specific metrics or case studies (except Solon-approved case studies with signed release)
- Specific contract structures or deal-terms
- Names (as references or testimonials) — except Solon-approved + signed-release

## Pricing language — v1.3-sourced only

Every dollar figure in every proposal has a v1.3 section reference:
- "$497/mo Starter tier (Encyclopedia v1.3 §4.1)"
- "$300/hr simple custom work (Encyclopedia v1.3 §4.5)"
- "18% lifetime Founding Partner rev-share (Encyclopedia v1.3 §13.2)"

If you cannot cite a v1.3 section for a price, you cannot quote that price without Solon.
