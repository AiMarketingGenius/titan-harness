## Platform playbooks (quick reference)

### Instagram (2026 current state)
- Reels drive reach > Feed static (5-7× typical). Target 60-70% of content as Reels.
- Carousel posts drive saves + shares (best for educational content).
- Stories for daily presence + behind-scenes.
- Collab posts (2-account Feed posts) doubled reach typically.
- Best post times vary by audience; default 11am + 7pm local.
- Hashtags: 5-8 relevant, not stuffed. Use Instagram Insights to find which of your hashtags drove reach.
- Links: only in bio (link-in-bio tools OK); story-swipe-up only for verified or 10K+ follower accounts; DM-link CTAs growing since 2025.

### Facebook
- 3-5 posts/week cadence.
- Native video gets priority in feed (5-10× text-only reach).
- Events + Offers drive action.
- Groups often higher-ROI than page posts for local businesses.
- Best times: weekday mornings for B2B; weekend evenings for B2C.
- Facebook Reels — test platform; meta pushing hard since 2024.

### LinkedIn
- 2-4 posts/week for individuals; daily possible for company pages with content-marketing investment.
- Long-form essays > short posts for reach (current algorithm).
- Hook in first 2 lines (mobile truncation).
- Comments drive reach more than likes.
- Best times: Tue-Thu 8-10am local.
- Polls = engagement spike; use 1-2× monthly max.
- LinkedIn Articles: use for thought-leadership; distribute as newsletter if subscriber has one.

### X (Twitter / formerly)
- 3-10 posts/day for active brand presence.
- Threads > single tweets for dwell time.
- Reply game > original post for relationship-building.
- Best times: audience-dependent; X Analytics shows actual peak-hours.
- Link in tweets suppressed in reach — use "link in bio" + first-reply-link pattern.

### TikTok
- 1-3 posts/day for growth phase; 3-5/week maintenance.
- Hook in first 3 seconds or viewer bounces.
- Native audio (trending or platform-licensed) outperforms custom.
- Post any time; algorithm weak time-weighted.
- Live streams 1-2× week for subscribers with community.

### Threads
- Daily posting if subscriber is active. Casual register.
- Cross-post opportunity with IG (Instagram-linked accounts).

## Content mix formulas

- **80/20 rule** — 80% value-first content (education, entertainment, community), 20% direct-promotional
- **Pillar + supporting** — 1 pillar (long-form, high effort) + 3-5 supporting (short-form, repurposed)
- **Behind-scenes / product / community / promotion** — rotate weekly

## Hashtag strategy

- **Branded (1-2)** — subscriber's own hashtag
- **Category (2-4)** — broad industry/niche tags
- **Geographic (1-2)** — local tags for local-business subscribers (#RevereMA, #LynnMA, #NorthShore)
- **Trending (0-1)** — only if genuinely fits; avoid bandwagoning
- **Campaign-specific (1-2)** — for active launches/promotions

Max 5-8 total on IG; 1-3 on LinkedIn; 2-4 on X; 0-2 on Facebook (emoji > hashtag on FB).

## Engagement-rate benchmarks (industry directional)

- **Instagram**: 1-3% engagement rate for business accounts under 10K; 4-7% possible with strong community
- **Facebook**: 0.5-2% for pages; groups much higher
- **LinkedIn**: 1-4% for personal posts; 0.5-2% for company pages
- **X**: 0.2-1% typical
- **TikTok**: 4-9% for growth-phase accounts
