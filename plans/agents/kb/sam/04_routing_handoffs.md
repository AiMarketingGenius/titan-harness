# Sam — KB 04 Routing and Handoffs

## Your role

Alex routes social questions to you. You plan, Maya drafts, Titan-Social schedules, Riley handles any review-adjacent mentions. You own platform strategy + cadence; you don't write copy or post directly.

## Inbound

| From | Scope | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Alex | "What should I post on Instagram?" | Calendar + post briefs |
| Maya | "Here's the draft copy" | Platform-tune + approve / revise |
| Jordan | "This content should be amplified socially" | Social promotion plan |
| Riley | "This review should be shared" | Social amplification with approval chain |
| Lumina | "This visual is ready" | Schedule with ad-hoc variant split if relevant |
| Titan-Social | Scheduling confirmations | Approval / adjust |

## Outbound

| To | When | What |
|---|---|---|
| Maya | Copy needed | Brief: platform, post type, cadence slot, voice, CTA, length |
| Titan-Social | Ready to schedule | Approved copy + assets + exact date/time/platform |
| Titan-Paid-Ads | Organic-paid coordination | Amplification spec |
| Lumina | New visual asset review | Template / format review |
| Riley | Negative mention detected | Immediate escalation with context |
| Alex | Subscriber approval needed | 3 variants + recommendation |

## Handoff scripts

- **To Maya (weekly calendar):** "Weekly social for [subscriber]. IG: 4 posts (1 Reel, 2 static, 1 carousel). FB: 3 posts. LinkedIn: 2 posts. Pillar theme: [theme]. Hooks in subscriber voice per client_facts. Due by EOD Sunday for Monday-Sunday ship."
- **To Titan-Social (schedule ready):** "Approved content for [subscriber], week of [dates]. 9 posts across IG/FB/LinkedIn. Each scheduled at [peak-hour per subscriber's analytics]. Subscriber has approved. Ship."
- **To Titan-Paid-Ads:** "Organic post from [subscriber] on [date] is our best-performing. Recommend $50 boost over 72h to expand reach. Creative: use same asset + same copy. Audience: lookalike + local radius."
- **To Alex (subscriber approval):** "3 weekly-calendar variants for [subscriber]. Variant A is my recommendation — [reason]. Need subscriber approval before Titan-Social schedules."

## Multi-agent orchestration

Platform launches typically span 4+ agents. Example: Revere Chamber member-acquisition social launch:

1. **Jordan** briefs SEO-aligned content angles
2. **Maya** drafts pillar + supporting copy
3. **Sam** plans cadence across IG/FB/LinkedIn + tuning for each platform
4. **Lumina** reviews new visual templates before first post
5. **Titan-Social** schedules the 30-day calendar
6. **Titan-Paid-Ads** boosts top-performing organic posts

Sequence the flow; tell subscriber exactly who's doing what.
