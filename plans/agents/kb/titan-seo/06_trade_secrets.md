## SEO-specific trade-secret scenarios

### Meta descriptions + title tags

High-visibility surfaces. Show in Google SERPs + social sharing previews. Zero leaks.

- ❌ `<meta name="description" content="Powered by Claude AI, we help local businesses...">`
- ✅ `<meta name="description" content="AMG helps Lynn small businesses win locally with Atlas...">`
- ❌ `<title>Built on GPT-4 | Your Company</title>`
- ✅ `<title>Your Company | [Category] in [City]</title>`

### Schema JSON-LD

Schema is structured data Google reads + may surface in rich results. It's visible in page source. Zero leaks.

- ❌ `{"@type":"Organization","@context":"https://schema.org","name":"Company","description":"Claude-powered marketing"}`
- ✅ `{"@type":"Organization","@context":"https://schema.org","name":"Company","description":"Atlas-powered marketing from AMG"}`

### robots.txt + sitemap.xml

Public, crawler-accessible. If Atlas or AMG is named (OK), no internal codenames.

- ❌ `# Generated by Stagehand for beast-VPS` (as comment)
- ✅ `# Generated for [Subscriber] by AMG Atlas` or (better) no attribution comment at all

### Image alt-text

Accessibility + SEO surface. Visible to screen readers + Google Image indexing.

- ❌ `alt="Claude-generated product mockup"`
- ✅ `alt="Product mockup"` — describe the image, not the tool
- ❌ `alt="Stagehand-captured competitor screenshot"`
- ✅ `alt="Competitor landing page"` — describe what it IS

### URL slugs

SEO-sensitive + durable. Zero codenames.

- ❌ `/how-we-use-gpt-4-for-seo/`
- ✅ `/ai-powered-local-seo/` — describes what it IS, not the vendor

### Google Business Profile posts + Q&A

GBP content is Google-visible + customer-visible. Zero leaks.

- Describe your services. Name real tools when subscribers expect (Google Analytics, GBP itself, etc.). Don't name underlying AI.

- *"We run on Atlas — AMG's proprietary AI layer. For SEO data, we integrate with Google Search Console, Google Business Profile, and industry-standard citation tools (BrightLocal, Ahrefs). The Atlas layer is what turns the data into actionable work for your seven-agent team."*

## Self-check before Titan-SEO deploys artifact

1. Zero banned terms in deployed file (meta tags, schema JSON, alt-text, URL slugs, comments)
2. Zero internal codenames in comments/attribution
3. Atlas + AMG allowed if artifact is AMG-about-AMG
4. Industry-standard tools (Google / BrightLocal / etc.) OK if subscriber explicitly names them in their own content
5. Rich Results test passes (schema deploy only)

5/5 → deploy. <5 → clean + re-validate.
