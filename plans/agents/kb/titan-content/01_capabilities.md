## Content-quality anti-patterns (auto-avoid)

- **"Unlock the power of AI"** and variants — generic SaaS boilerplate
- **"Are you ready to..."** openers — yes/no reader bail
- **"Here at [company], we believe..."** — corporate-about-page register
- **"In today's fast-paced world..."** — immediate filler
- **"Revolutionize your business"** — undefined promise
- **Passive voice overuse** — "this was done" → "we did this"
- **Adverb stacking** — "really truly significantly meaningfully impactful" → "impactful" or (better) specific
- **Vague CTA** — "Learn more" → "Get your free GBP audit" or other specific action
- **Feature-dump** — listing 5 features instead of one clear value-prop

## Content-quality exemplars (what Maya wants)

- **Specificity over abstraction:** "The Pioli brothers press Friday slice night at the same 6pm start since 1978" > "The family continues their legacy of weekend dinners"
- **Action over description:** "Book a booth before July 4" > "Consider reserving a table for the holiday"
- **Time-stamped claims:** "Your Reels cadence dropped from 4/week to 1/week on April 3" > "Your posting has been inconsistent lately"
- **Names + places + dates:** "Don at the Revere Chamber" > "Local leaders"
- **Numbers with sources:** "Google Search Console shows your top keyword 'Lynn real estate' dropped from position 4 to 11 on April 9" > "Your rankings decreased"

## Self-check before submitting to Maya

1. Voice sample adopted or flagged missing?
2. Zero banned terms (agency boilerplate, AI-assistant register, trade-secret leaks)?
3. Specifics (names, places, dates, numbers) where they could appear?
4. Clear action / next step for reader?
5. Length appropriate to surface (platform char-count, blog word-count, email brevity)?
6. Reading level appropriate to audience?

6/6 → submit to Maya. <6 → revise before submission.
