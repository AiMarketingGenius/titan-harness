## 2. Two Boats framework (WEAVES INTO SITE COPY — §3.x on /chamber-ai-advantage)

Every Chamber reaches the same fork:

### Boat 1 — Traditional vendor relationship

- Chamber pays vendor (monthly/annually)
- Chamber absorbs cost; Board sees expense line
- Member benefit is indirect (internal efficiency)
- Vendor owns the member relationship after introduction
- Relationship ends when contract ends

### Boat 2 — Channel partnership (Chamber AI Advantage)

- Chamber earns margin on every active member subscription (Layer 2)
- Chamber adds non-dues revenue; Board sees income line
- Member benefit is direct (members get AI marketing service at Chamber-member rate)
- Chamber owns the branded member relationship permanently
- Relationship compounds as member base grows

**Closing line (site-safe, can appear in bold):** *"Boat 1 is a budget line. Boat 2 is an engine."*

## 4. Risk-reversal clauses (for Founding Partner page FAQ + application pitch)

Per Encyclopedia §13 Legal Framework + §6 Exclusive Territory:

- **Channel-first contractual commitment** (§3.1): AMG agrees not to direct-sell Layer 2 product to individual businesses in partnered territories. Chamber trust lives here.
- **Exclusive Territory quota** (§6): if a Chamber purchases Exclusive Territory, annual quota applies. Miss quota → 30-day cure period → if still missed, territory returns to non-exclusive; Chamber retains existing member relationships + rev-share on those.
- **Cross-state anti-poaching** (§6.5): Chambers in other states cannot recruit members out of a partner Chamber's state territory. Contract breach triggers termination + clawback on poached accounts.

## 7. Channel-partner objection handlers (for Alex, not on site)

**Q: "How is this different from the 20 vendors pitching my Chamber?"**
A: Traditional vendors SELL the Chamber a tool the Chamber pays for. AMG's Chamber AI Advantage inverts that — AMG pays the Chamber a share of every member subscription sold through the channel. The Chamber's budget isn't a cost center; it's a revenue line.

**Q: "We already have ChamberMaster/GrowthZone/MemberClicks."**
A: Complementary, not competitive. AMS platforms run Chamber operations (roster, billing, events). Chamber AI Advantage runs the member-revenue layer — AI marketing services delivered as a Chamber-branded benefit. Chambers often run both.

**Q: "What's the risk if members don't adopt?"**
A: Layer 2 (member platform) is zero platform cost to the Chamber. No minimums, no lock-in. Layer 1 (website rebuild) is a one-time investment that's valuable regardless of Layer 2 adoption. The Chamber's downside is Layer 1 + Chamber OS spend without Layer 2 rev-share to offset — which is why AMG includes the launch webinar, co-op marketing match, and Board-ready member communications template.

**Q: "What if another Chamber near us also does this?"**
A: Default territory is 20-mile non-exclusive radius. If you want hard exclusivity, Exclusive Territory buy-up (§6) is available — credits back against your own rev-share earnings, so if you hit your quota it's effectively free. Cross-state anti-poaching is already contractual across all partners.
