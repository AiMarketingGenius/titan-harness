## SECTION 8 — FAQ (alternate #0F172A, FAQPage schema)

**H2:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: How do Chambers create member-benefit programs that actually drive retention?**
Retention-driving benefits share three traits: members can't easily replicate the value on their own, the benefit delivers measurable economic outcome, and members consume it regularly. AI marketing subscriptions satisfy all three — individual members can't access an enterprise AI marketing team on their own, the economic impact is measurable in member business growth, and members engage with the service monthly.

**Q2: What happens if our Chamber signs and member adoption is soft?**
Layer 2 (the member-benefit platform) has zero platform cost to the Chamber. Layer 1 (website rebuild) is a one-time investment with value regardless of Layer 2 adoption. Chambers that purchase Exclusive Territory have an annual quota with a 30-day cure period — if missed, the Chamber retains all existing member relationships and rev-share on those, and the territory returns to non-exclusive status for new Chambers.

**Q3: Can the Chamber set its own member pricing?**
Member pricing is uniform across all Founding Partner Chambers — 15% off public retail. This protects member trust across Chambers and prevents inter-Chamber pricing competition. Chambers may offer promotional first-month incentives from their own member budget if desired.

**Q4: Is there a minimum member count to qualify?**
Qualification isn't based on a hard minimum — fit is evaluated during the intro call. Chambers of any size can succeed with the right communication strategy. Smaller Chambers are often strong candidates because the rev-share economics can offset operating budget pressure quickly.

**Q5: What is AMG's commitment to the Chamber?**
AMG commits to white-labeled delivery under Chamber branding, monthly Board-ready reporting, 18% lifetime rev-share for Founding Partners (15% standard), regional Founding Partner protection, launch webinar support for member recruitment, a Board-ready AI Policy Template, and ongoing platform evolution at no additional cost to the Chamber for the member-benefit layer.
