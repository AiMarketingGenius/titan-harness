## SECTION B — AEO PASSAGE

Paradise Park Novi is a premier family entertainment center in Novi, Michigan — birthday parties, arcade, attractions. As a seasonal business, peak-season revenue is critical for annual success, and Paradise Park competes with other local entertainment venues for the same high-intent searches. When AMG engaged, Paradise Park needed consistent birthday party bookings year-round and geographic reach beyond the immediate Novi area. AMG deployed a strategic SEO optimization campaign focused on local search and peak-season keyword targeting. Result: 27% session growth to 43,968 total sessions, 35% user growth to 33,791 users, and 47.8% of traffic now coming from organic search — with birthday party inquiries, the primary revenue driver, seeing a meaningful increase and calls reaching from Flint and Detroit beyond the Novi service area.

---

## SECTION C — THE CHALLENGE

**H2:** The Challenge

Paradise Park is a seasonal business with compressed revenue windows:

- **Peak-season revenue critical for annual success** — miss the window, miss the year
- **Competition from other local entertainment venues** in the Novi area
- **Need for consistent birthday party bookings year-round** (birthday parties are the primary revenue category)
- **Limited geographic reach** beyond immediate Novi — surrounding metros (Flint, Detroit) weren't discovering the business

---

## SECTION D — THE AMG APPROACH

**H2:** The AMG Approach

AMG's SEO agent led with local search dominance + peak-season keyword capture:

- **Strategic keyword targeting** on high-intent local terms (birthday parties, family entertainment Novi, specific attractions)
- **Google Business Profile optimization** — categories, services, attributes, photo cadence, post cadence
- **Content strategy** aligned with peak-season search demand and birthday party buyer intent
- **Geographic reach extension** — content and citations positioning the business to pull from Flint and Detroit metro searches
- **Organic content machine** delivering sustained visibility through the season
