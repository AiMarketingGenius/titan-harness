## SECTION B — AEO PASSAGE (max-width 780px)

Shop UNIS is a B2B wholesale supplier of licensed plush toys, novelties, and arcade prizes — Funko, Squid Game, Disney, Tokidoki, Emoji — serving Family Entertainment Center operators, arcades, and amusement centers across the United States and Canada. When AMG engaged in August 2025, Shop UNIS had zero search visibility for high-value wholesale keywords, 611 technical SEO issues on the Shopify platform, and was competing against established players (Rhode Island Novelty, BMI Merchandise) in a highly niche B2B market. Six months later, sessions grew from 1,017 to 4,562 per month (+348%), users jumped from 492 to 2,738 (+456%), and US market sessions exploded from 440 to 2,324 (+428%). Sales followed the traffic — especially in the primary US market — and Shop UNIS renewed for a second year.

---

## SECTION C — THE CHALLENGE

**H2:** The Challenge

Shop UNIS faced four compounding problems:

- **Zero search visibility** for high-value wholesale keywords in a niche B2B market
- **Entrenched competitors** (Rhode Island Novelty, BMI Merchandise) dominating the top positions
- **Complex licensing requirements** limiting content flexibility (Funko, Disney, Squid Game each carry usage restrictions)
- **611 technical SEO issues** flagged on the Shopify platform baseline audit, pulling down the site's 83/100 health score

For a B2B operator, niche-intent traffic is the lifeblood. Without it, the sales team has no warm inbound.

---

## SECTION D — THE AMG APPROACH

**H2:** The AMG Approach — Three Pillars

**1. Technical foundation**
Complete site audit surfaced 611 technical issues. AMG's SEO agent prioritized them by impact-vs-effort and deployed Shopify-specific optimizations. Platform health score climbed from 83/100 baseline toward a clean baseline.

**2. Strategic keyword targeting**
AMG's SEO agent identified 8 high-value buyer-intent wholesale keywords, then ran competitor gap analysis against Rhode Island Novelty and BMI Merchandise to find terms where Shop UNIS could realistically rank in 2 quarters. Licensed-line product pages got targeted optimization per brand (Funko, Squid Game, Disney, Tokidoki).

**3. Content and authority building**
AMG's content agent launched a weekly B2B-focused article schedule aligned with the FEC/arcade operator buyer persona. AMG's outbound agent ran niche-relevant backlink acquisition. Product pages per licensed line were rewritten for conversion + search.
