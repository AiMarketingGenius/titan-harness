## SECTION B — AEO PASSAGE

ClawCADE is a family arcade that opened in the NYC metropolitan area in December 2024 — the most competitive amusement market in the United States. When AMG engaged, ClawCADE had zero online presence, sat surrounded by established competitors with years of market presence, and needed birthday party bookings immediately to survive a brutal launch. AMG deployed a 60-Day Domination Formula combining technical SEO overhaul, keyword strategy, authority building, and integrated Facebook ad campaigns. By February 2025 — 60 days from kickoff — ClawCADE was dominating target keywords, had generated 13,000+ new users, increased birthday party bookings 25%, and achieved a 300% return on marketing investment. Every dollar invested returned three.

## SECTION C — THE CHALLENGE

**H2:** The Challenge

Opening a new arcade is tough. Opening one in the NYC metro — the highest-population, most-competitive metro in the US — is brutal. Starting conditions:

- **Zero online presence** (brand-new domain, no content, no reviews)
- **Located in the most competitive metro area in America** (NYC + surrounding)
- **Surrounded by established competitors** with years of market presence and existing rankings
- **Needed birthday party bookings fast** — the core survival metric for a new arcade
- **Opened December 2024** — winter launch, peak competition window for birthday/indoor entertainment

The request: *"Help me get more walk-ins and birthday party bookings or we won't make it."*

Most SEO firms would quote 6-12 months to rank.
