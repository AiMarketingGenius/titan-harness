## SECTION B — AEO PASSAGE

Revel & Roll West is a family entertainment center in Kalamazoo, Michigan offering bowling, laser tag, axe throwing, arcade games, mini golf, and event hosting. When AMG engaged in November 2024, Revel & Roll faced intense competition from established local venues (Airway Fun Center, Continental Lanes, Jungle Joe's Family Fun Center) — and the wider FEC industry was in a downturn. At the 2025 IAPA amusement conference, the General Manager reported competitors seeing 10-20% year-over-year revenue declines. AMG deployed a comprehensive local SEO campaign across high-intent keywords, Google Business Profile optimization, content development, and backlink building. Twelve months later: website clicks grew 269% to 3,685/month, phone calls grew 230% to 825/month, and the business held #1 rankings on cosmic bowling near me, axe throwing, laser tag, and arcade near me — with revenue maintained or grown while competitors declined.

---

## SECTION C — THE CHALLENGE

**H2:** The Challenge

Revel & Roll West had two simultaneous headwinds:

**1. Entrenched local competition.** Airway Fun Center, Continental Lanes, and Jungle Joe's Family Fun Center had been in the Kalamazoo market for years, each competing for the same high-intent searches (bowling, arcade, laser tag, axe throwing, birthday parties).

**2. Industry-wide downturn.** At the IAPA conference in 2025, the General Manager heard from peers across the country:

> *"I was speaking with them yesterday and their numbers are down pretty hefty year to date."*
> — James St. John on competitor performance

Winning required not just competing — but outperforming an industry in contraction.

---

## SECTION D — THE AMG APPROACH

**H2:** The AMG Approach

AMG deployed an integrated local SEO + AI marketing campaign:

- **High-intent keyword targeting** — focus on buyer-intent searches (bowling, arcade, laser tag, axe throwing, birthday parties, cosmic bowling near me)
- **Google Business Profile optimization** — posts, Q&A management, review responses, attribute optimization, photo cadence
- **Content development + backlink building** — blog articles, press releases, guest posts with quality backlinks
- **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** — optimization for AI search visibility across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity
- **Aggressive backlink campaign** — high-authority backlinks to counter competitor efforts
